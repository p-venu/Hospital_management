About the Cardiology Department

The Cardiology Department at NBKR Hospital specializes in diagnosing and treating heart-related conditions, offering cutting-edge diagnostics, interventional procedures, and 24/7 emergency cardiac care. Our team of expert cardiologists and support staff is dedicated to improving patient outcomes through personalized treatment plans and state-of-the-art technology.
